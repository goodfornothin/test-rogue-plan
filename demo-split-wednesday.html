Good to know

Frequently Asked Questions

No partner needed, beginners welcome, £10 for the class with a free social after — and it's genuinely safe to come alone. Details below.

Do I need a partner to come?

No. Most people come alone, and we rotate partners throughout the class, so you'll dance with everyone in the room by the end of the hour.

Is it okay to come completely alone?

Yes — it's genuinely safe to come alone, and most people do. You'll be welcomed the moment you walk in, paired up for the class, and it's a great way to meet new friends without needing to bring anyone.

Is this for complete beginners?

Yes — designed from the ground up for absolute beginners. If you've never danced before, this is the perfect place to start, and you'll never be the only first-timer in the room.

How much does it cost?

The class is £10. Social dancing afterwards, from 8:30pm, is completely free.

Where is it?

Keystone Crescent, King's Cross, London N1 9DX — about a 2 minute walk from King's Cross / St Pancras stations.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothes you can move in. Smooth or leather-soled shoes work best — avoid thick rubber-soled trainers if you can.

Do I need to book in advance?

Booking is recommended — the class fills up — but doors also take walk-ins. The free social from 8:30pm needs no booking at all.
