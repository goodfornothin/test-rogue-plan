Rogue Bachata Workshop

An immersive experience into the world of Bachata.

Workshop Details

This workshop is designed for dancers of all levels who want to deepen their understanding of Bachata. We will focus on:

  • Connection with your partner
  • Musicality and interpretation
  • Improvisation and creativity
  • Advanced techniques and patterns

About Rogue Resonance

Rogue Resonance is a series of workshops designed to explore the depths of partner dancing, focusing on connection, musicality, and authentic expression. Each workshop is a unique experience, offering a space to learn, grow, and connect with yourself and others.
