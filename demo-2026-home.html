How we teach

The Rogue Principles

Six principles form the backbone of everything we teach — from your first beginner class to teacher training — all built on listening to your partner rather than performing at them.

Rogue Body, Bond, Beyond

Ground in yourself, listen to your partner, expand into the space

Rogue Legato

Your center of gravity travels on a continuous, unbroken path

Rogue Pause, Presence, Pivot

Stop the movement, find each other, then redirect together

Rogue Bounce and Roll

When bodies meet, rebound elastically or redirect

Rogue Humility

Surrender your movement when connection is lost; seek the partner before continuing

Rogue Lazy

Relaxed and lazy, yet in time—a paradox found in both music and dance

Good to know

Frequently Asked Questions

The short version: no partner needed, beginners welcome, £10 a class with free socials, and performances from £500 — details below.

Do I need a partner?

No — you don't need a partner. Most people come alone and we rotate partners throughout every class, so you'll dance with everyone.

I'm a complete beginner — is that OK?

Yes. Both weekly nights are beginner-friendly and no experience is needed. We teach from the ground up, and there are always other first-timers in the room.

How much does it cost?

The class on both nights is £10, and the social dancing afterwards is completely free. There's no membership and nothing to commit to.

Where are the two venues?

Tuesdays are at Cafe Sol, 56 Clapham High Street, London SW4 7UL. Wednesdays are at Keystone Crescent, King's Cross, London N1 9DX — about two minutes' walk from King's Cross station.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothes you can move in. Smooth or leather-soled shoes work best — avoid thick rubber-soled trainers if you can.

How do I book a class?

For Wednesdays, book through the Fatsoma links on this page or pay at the door. For Tuesdays at Cafe Sol, just come along — arrive a little before 7:30pm.

How do performances work and what do they cost?

A professional opera singer sings live while dancing bachata with his partner — a set of a few songs plus entertainment, from £500. Message us on WhatsApp or email voice@oscarcastellino.com with your date and venue for a quote.

Do you do private lessons or group bookings?

Yes. We offer private and small-group sessions — bring two leaders and two followers to split the cost — plus group and corporate packages, with group rates for 10 or more people.
