Frequently asked

Do I need a partner?

No. Most people come alone. We rotate partners throughout the class.

Is this for complete beginners?

Yes — designed from the ground up for absolute beginners. If you've never danced before, this is the perfect place to start.

How much does it cost?

The 1-hour class is £10. Social dancing from 8:30pm is completely free.

Where is it?

Keystone Crescent, King's Cross, London N1 9DX. 2 min walk from King's Cross / St Pancras stations.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothes. Smooth or leather-soled shoes work best — avoid thick rubber-soled trainers.

Do I need to book?

Booking is recommended for the class — it fills up. The free social from 8:30pm needs no booking.
