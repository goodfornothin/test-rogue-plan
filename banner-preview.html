Time-bound logic (JS) — how it works:

  • "Café Sol tonight" card/message → hidden after midnight 1 Apr 2026
  • "Easter Monday TBC" → hidden after 23:59 on 6 Apr 2026
  • "Next confirmed 13 Apr" → hidden after 23:59 on 13 Apr 2026
  • If all messages expire → entire banner/tab hides automatically
  • Dismissal (✕ button) → stored in sessionStorage, won't reappear until next visit
