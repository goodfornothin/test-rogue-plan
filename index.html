How we teach

The Rogue Principles

Six principles form the backbone of everything we teach — from your first beginner class to teacher training — all built on listening to your partner rather than performing at them.

Rogue Body, Bond, Beyond

Ground in yourself, listen to your partner, expand into the space

Rogue Legato

Your center of gravity travels on a continuous, unbroken path

Rogue Pause, Presence, Pivot

Stop the movement, find each other, then redirect together

Rogue Bounce and Roll

When bodies meet, rebound elastically or redirect

Rogue Humility

Surrender your movement when connection is lost; seek the partner before continuing

Rogue Lazy

Relaxed and lazy, yet in time—a paradox found in both music and dance

Good to know

Frequently Asked Questions

The short version: no partner needed, beginners welcome, £10 a class with free socials, and it's genuinely safe to come alone — details below.

Do I need a partner to come?

No partner needed, no experience, no pressure. Most people come solo and we rotate partners throughout the class, so you'll dance with everyone in the room.

Is it okay to come completely alone?

Yes — it's genuinely safe to come alone, and most people do. You'll be welcomed the moment you walk in, paired up for the class, and it's a great way to meet new friends without needing to bring anyone.

What's the vibe like for total beginners?

Warm and no-judgment. You will never be the only beginner in the room — every class is full of first-timers, and the whole hour is built from zero in a welcoming, relaxed atmosphere.

How much does it cost?

£10 for the class on both nights. Social dancing afterwards, from 8:30pm, is completely free.

Where are Tuesday and Wednesday nights held?

Tuesdays are at Cafe Sol, 56 Clapham High Street, London SW4 7UL. Wednesdays are at Keystone Crescent, King's Cross, London N1 9DX.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothes you can move in. Smooth or leather-soled shoes work best — avoid thick rubber-soled trainers if you can.

Do I need to book in advance?

Booking is recommended for Wednesdays — use the link on this page or pay at the door. Tuesdays at Cafe Sol are pay-on-the-door, just come along a little before 7:30pm.
