The Rogue Principles
  • Rogue Body, Bond, Beyond — Ground in yourself, listen to your partner, expand into the space.
  • Rogue Legato — Your center of gravity travels on a continuous, unbroken path.
  • Rogue Pause, Presence, Pivot — Stop the movement, find each other, then redirect together.
  • Rogue Bounce and Roll — When bodies meet, rebound elastically or redirect.
  • Rogue Humility — Surrender your movement when connection is lost; seek the partner before continuing.
  • Rogue Lazy — Relaxed and lazy, yet in time — a paradox found in both music and dance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it okay to come completely alone?

Completely. Most people turn up solo — you'll be rotated through partners in class and folded straight into the group. No one will let you stand on the sidelines.

What's the atmosphere like for total beginners?

Warm and unhurried. We teach from zero, nobody's watching for mistakes, and the regulars are there to help, not judge. It's the friendliest room in London to try something new.

Do I need a partner to attend?

No partner needed for either night. We rotate throughout the class so everyone dances with everyone.

How much does it cost?

£10 for the class on both nights. Social dancing afterwards is completely free.

Where are the classes held?

Tuesdays at Cafe Sol, 56 Clapham High Street SW4 7UL. Wednesdays at Keystone Crescent, King's Cross N1 9DX.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothes and smooth-soled shoes. Trainers with thick rubber soles grip the floor too much — heels are optional.
