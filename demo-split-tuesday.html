Good to know

Frequently Asked Questions

The short version: no partner needed, beginners welcome, £10 a class with a free social after, and it's genuinely safe to come alone — details below.

Do I need a partner to come?

No partner needed, no experience, no pressure. Most people come solo and we rotate partners throughout the class, so you'll dance with everyone in the room.

Is it okay to come completely alone?

Yes — it's genuinely safe to come alone, and most people do. You'll be welcomed the moment you walk in, paired up for the class, and it's a great way to meet new friends without needing to bring anyone.

What's the vibe like for total beginners?

Warm and no-judgment. You will never be the only beginner in the room — every class is full of first-timers, and the whole hour is built from zero in a welcoming, relaxed atmosphere.

How much does it cost?

£10 for the class. Social dancing afterwards, from 8:30pm until late, is completely free — and you can turn up for just the social with no booking needed.

Where exactly is it?

Cafe Sol, 56 Clapham High Street, London SW4 7UL — a short walk from Clapham North tube station (Northern line) and Clapham High Street Overground.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothes you can move in. Smooth or leather-soled shoes work best — avoid thick rubber-soled trainers if you can.

Do I need to book in advance?

Booking is recommended to secure your spot in the class, but Cafe Sol is pay-on-the-door too — just come along a little before 7:30pm. No booking is needed at all if you're only joining the free social.
