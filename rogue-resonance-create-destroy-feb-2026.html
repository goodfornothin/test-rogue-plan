About This Workshop

Join Oscar for an immersive Rogue Resonance workshop at Create Destroy Studios. This session will explore the core principles of Rogue Resonance‚Äîgroundedness, flow, and authentic connection.

This workshop is suitable for individuals with at least a few months of bachata experience, OR those with experience in another dance form, OR anyone enthusiastic and willing to experiment. The most important quality is a willingness to try!

What You'll Explore

  • Groundedness: Finding stability and presence in your own body
  • Flow: Moving with ease and efficiency through space
  • Authentic Connection: Creating meaningful partnership moments
  • Bounce and Roll: The elasticity of bodies meeting in partnership
  • Stop and Connect: Pausing to find presence before moving again

Core Rogue Principles Covered

Body, Bond, Beyond

Ground in yourself, listen to your partner, expand into the space

Legato

Your center of gravity travels on a continuous, unbroken path

Pause, Presence, Pivot

Stop the movement, find each other, then redirect together

Bounce and Roll

When bodies meet, rebound elastically or redirect

Humility

Surrender your movement when connection is lost; seek the partner first

Lazy

Relaxed and lazy, yet in time‚Äîa paradox found in both music and dance

Who Should Attend?

  • Dancers with a few months of bachata experience
  • Experienced dancers from other dance styles
  • Anyone curious and willing to explore movement and connection
  • Dancers seeking to deepen their understanding of partner connection
