Not about the steps

Dance as Connection, Not Performance

Partner dance is one of the most intimate and connecting activities a couple can share. It's not about nailing choreography — it's about learning to listen to each other, exactly like every class we teach.

Sessions happen in the comfort of your own home — no crowded studios, no judgment, just the two of you. All you need is a small space where you can move freely; we bring the music, the expertise, and the patience. Every session is tailored to your experience level, goals, and the kind of connection you want to build — no dance experience needed.

Good to know

Frequently Asked Questions

The short version: no experience needed, sessions happen in your home, and rates are tailored to what you need — details below.

Do we need any dance experience?

None at all. Every session is tailored to your experience level, goals, and the kind of connection you want to build — this is about you two, not a performance.

How long is a session?

Sessions are approximately 90 minutes.

Where do sessions take place?

In the comfort of your own home — no crowded studios, no judgment, just the two of you. All you need is a small space where you can move freely; we bring the music, the expertise, and the patience.

What happens first?

We start with a brief initial consultation — a chat to understand your goals, experience level, and what you hope to achieve through dance — so your first session is tailored from the start.

How much does it cost?

Rates are tailored to your session — get in touch and we'll talk through what fits, whether that's a single session, weekly, or bi-weekly.
